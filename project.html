Project Name: The Urban Brew Co.

We had the privilege of creating a new brand identity package for "The Urban Brew Co." The goal was to give their cafe a modern, stylish look that would resonate with their target audience of young, urban customers.

Our Approach

We began by understanding the core values of "The Urban Brew Co." to create a branding strategy that truly matched their unique vibe. Our focus was on clean lines and a simple aesthetic.

The services we provided for this project included:

Logo Design: We crafted a minimalist logo that cleverly blends the image of coffee beans with a city skyline, reflecting both the product and its urban setting.

Branding Collateral: We designed a full suite of branded materials, including business cards, menus, and coasters, ensuring a consistent and professional look across all touchpoints.

Packaging Design: We created a distinctive and eye-catching design for their takeaway coffee cups and bags to stand out from the competition.

Results

Our design gave "The Urban Brew Co." a fresh, consistent, and memorable brand identity. This has not only enhanced their market presence but has also been a major hit with their customers, who appreciate the new look and feel.
