About us

Wellmade_22 was officially launched in 2022, but the idea was born much earlier, during the COVID-19 pandemic. The concept came to founder Bhavik Sharma when he was just in 10th grade. During that time of stillness, he recognized a need for creativity and quality in products, and from that observation, the idea for Wellmade_22 took shape. This isn't just a business; it's the result of years of planning and hard work. We bring that same initial passion and dedication to every project, offering top-notch graphic design, printing, and custom gifts to our clients.
